Novia Ng

The Life of Novo

Born in the eastern Ukrainian city of Donetsk, Novie was the youngest graduate of the Donbass School of Wearable and Werkable Arts at just 21. The influence of her colourful Slavic and Cuban heritage is evident in her work. The flamboyant and at times Piacassian, Novio's pysankic designs can be viewed at the Centre Pompidou and MOMA and of course.
